How to use the CRM

Your CRM keeps track of the people you deal with and the deals you're working on — in one place, private to your business. It has two tabs: Contacts and Pipeline.

📇 Contacts

Your address book — every person and company you work with.

  1. Add a contact. On the Contacts tab, click + Add. Enter a name (the only required field), choose Person or Company, and add email, phone, company or notes. Click Save.
  2. Find a contact. Use the Search box — it filters instantly by name, company, or email as you type.
  3. Edit or remove. Click any row to reopen it, make your changes and Save — or click Delete.

📊 Pipeline

Your deals, moving left to right as they progress. Each column is a stage; its header shows how many deals are in it and their total value.

⇢ Drag a card from one column to the next as the deal moves forward — it saves automatically.
  1. Add a deal. On the Pipeline tab, click + Add. Give it a title, a value in £, pick the stage it's at, and (optionally) link a contact. Click Save — it appears as a card.
  2. Move it forward. Drag the card into the next column as things progress. There's nothing to save — it updates on its own.
  3. Win or lose it. Click the card and set Status to Won or Lost. Won deals get a green edge; lost ones fade — so the board stays honest at a glance.
  4. Edit or remove. Click any card to reopen, change the details and Save, or Delete.

Good to know

  • It's private to your business. Only you and your team see your contacts and deals.
  • You start ready to go. The pipeline comes pre-built with the usual stages — Lead, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal, Won, Lost — so you can add your first deal straight away.
  • Link deals to contacts. Attaching a contact to a deal shows who it's with, right on the card.

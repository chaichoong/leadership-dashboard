🎯 1. Purpose & Scope

The Profit & Loss OS provides a real-time view of business financial performance, derived directly from reconciled transactions in Airtable. It enables the business owner to monitor revenue, costs, and margins by month, identify trends via AI-powered analysis, and correct mis-categorised transactions without leaving the report.

ℹ️
Scope: Covers Revenue, Cost of Goods Sold, and Operating Expenses for the Real Estate and Operations Director businesses. Capital expenditure, loans, transfers, balance-sheet, and personal transactions are explicitly excluded.

🎯 2. Financial Targets

Metric Monthly Target Notes
Revenue £35,000/mo Total income across all revenue sub-categories
Gross Profit Margin 80% Gross Profit ÷ Revenue × 100
Net Profit £5,000/mo Gross Profit minus all Operating Expenses
Net Profit Margin 15% Baseline target — the primary margin KPI
Maintenance Budget £3,000/mo COGS Property Reactive Maintenance
Wages Budget £1,500/mo COGS Labour + Opex Labour combined
💡
KPI cards show green ▲ when above target (profit metrics) or below budget (cost metrics), and red ▼ when the opposite. Maintenance and wages also display their percentage of revenue.

📥 3. Data Source & Filters

All figures are computed from the Transactions table in the Operations Director Airtable base, filtered by:

Filter Field Values
Business Business (For Reports) Real Estate or Operations Director (dropdown at top of report)
Period Date 1, 3, 6, or 12 trailing months (dropdown at top of report)
Classification Chart of Accounts Sub-Category 27 sub-categories in the allow-list (see Section 6)
ℹ️
Key field: The report uses Report Amount (not raw GBP Amount) so that multi-currency transactions are normalised.

🔍 5. Drill-Down & Editing

Clicking any non-zero value in the P&L grid opens a modal showing all matching transactions:

Column Editable Notes
Date No Transaction date from Airtable
Transaction Detail No Vendor / description
Report Amount No Colour-coded: green positive, red negative
Category Yes Type-to-search — Chart of Accounts Category
Sub-Category Yes Type-to-search — Chart of Accounts Sub-Category
Business (For Reports) Yes Type-to-search — includes Personal for corrections
⚠️
Changes save immediately to Airtable. You’ll see ✓ when saved or ✗ on error. When you close the modal, the P&L re-renders with updated data.

📋 6. Sub-Category Classification

✅ 7. Best Practices

  1. Reconcile transactions first — the P&L only includes transactions with a sub-category and business link. Use the Leadership Dashboard reconciliation tab to categorise new transactions.
  2. Check the Business filter — ensure transactions are assigned to the correct business (Real Estate vs Operations Director). Use the drill-down to correct any that are wrong.
  3. Use AI Analysis regularly — generate an analysis at least monthly to catch trends early and track progress toward the 15% net margin target.
  4. Focus on margin, not just revenue — the 80% gross margin and 15% net margin targets are more important than raw revenue growth.
  5. Compare periods — switch between 3-month and 12-month views to distinguish seasonal patterns from genuine trends.
  6. Monitor maintenance and wages — these are the two budget-tracked line items. Check their percentage of revenue to spot cost creep.

🛠️ 8. Troubleshooting

❌ P&L shows “Loading transactions…”
✅ Open the Leadership Dashboard tab first to load transaction data, then switch back to P&L.
❌ A transaction is in the wrong category
✅ Click the value to drill down, then use the type-to-search inputs to correct Category, Sub-Category, or Business. Changes save to Airtable immediately.
❌ Revenue or expense is missing from the report
✅ Check that the transaction in Airtable has both a Sub-Category and Business (For Reports) link. Unlinked transactions are skipped.
❌ AI Analysis fails or shows an error
✅ Check internet connection and click Retry. The Claude AI proxy may be temporarily unavailable.
❌ Charts not rendering or showing blank
✅ Hard-refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R) to reload Chart.js from CDN.
❌ Drill-down edit shows “✗ no match”
✅ The typed name didn’t match any record. Check spelling or pick from the dropdown suggestions.
